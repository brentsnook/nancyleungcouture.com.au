Jamie Lee

Description

Jamie is pure glamour. When Jamie walks by, she captivates everybody’s attention. Sophisticated and statuesque.

Jamie’s long line strapless bodice is ruched, the design lines enhance the natural curves of a woman’s figure. A sparkle of hand beaded crystals and diamontes around the sweetheart neckline draw the eyes upward, elongating the figure. The gentle mermaid skirt is a soft silky tulle.
